About Me

For as long as I can remember, I always wanted to work with computers. Computers always amazed me and brought out my inner child and imagination. I’ve always wondered how people could sit on their desk and just travel to a different world for hours and I only knew how to read online news and check email. Now all of that has changed. I don’t have enough time in my day to work on new projects and show my friends and classmates what I have learned. Coding has changed the way I see things, not only in the virtual world, but in reality, as well. I still have a long, challenging road ahead of me, but with the help of my friends and family I would come out victorious.
